About Us

About Us

In the heart of Gombe State, nestled in North Eastern Nigeria, JuneTag emerged from a profound passion for creating delightful experiences through baking, tailoring, and cooking. Founded by June, a skilled baker with a talent for transforming basic ingredients into exquisite cakes and pastries, JuneTag quickly became renowned for its delectable treats. June's commitment to quality and innovation attracted a devoted following of food enthusiasts and dessert aficionados, establishing JuneTag as a prominent culinary destination in the city. Driven by a desire to extend her creative endeavors beyond the kitchen, June expanded JuneTag's offerings to include tailor-made outfits. Collaborating with adept tailors, JuneTag began offering custom-tailored garments that seamlessly blended style, comfort, and elegance. This expansion marked a pivotal moment for JuneTag, as it evolved into a multifaceted establishment that catered to both culinary and sartorial needs, captivating the hearts of locals and visitors alike with its distinctive offerings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services for your baked goods and tailor-made outfits?

Yes, we offer delivery services for both our baked goods and tailor-made outfits. Our delivery team ensures that your orders are delivered fresh and on time, straight to your doorstep. Delivery fees may vary based on the distance and size of the order.

Can I place a custom order for a cake or outfit that is not listed on your menu or catalog?

Absolutely! We love creating custom cakes and outfits that cater to your unique preferences and requirements. Simply reach out to us with your ideas, and our team will work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

Are your baked goods and dishes suitable for dietary restrictions such as gluten-free, dairy-free, or vegan?

Yes, we understand the importance of catering to various dietary needs. We offer a selection of gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan options for both our baked goods and dishes. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when placing your order.

Do you provide catering services for events such as weddings, parties, and corporate functions?

Yes, we offer catering services for a wide range of events, including weddings, parties, and corporate functions. Our experienced team can create a customized menu tailored to your event's theme and dietary requirements. Contact us for more information and to discuss your catering needs.
